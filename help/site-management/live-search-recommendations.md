# [!DNL Live Search] for recommendations

{{ee-feature}}

[!DNL Live Search] from Adobe Commerce delivers a fast, super-relevant, and intuitive search experience for Adobe Commerce at no additional charge. [!DNL Live Search] powered by Adobe AI uses artificial intelligence and machine-learning algorithms to perform a deep analysis of aggregated visitor data. This data, when combined with your Adobe Commerce catalog, results in highly engaging, relevant, and personalized shopping experiences. This video helps explain how to use live search to power product recommendations and was part of an ongoing webinar series - &quot;Commerce quick wins&quot;.
